Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision

Project Mind Matters was founded with a clear vision: to create a world where mental health is prioritized and everyone has access to the support they need.

We believe in a future where mental wellness is understood, destigmatized, and accessible to all individuals regardless of their background or circumstances.

Our Mission

Our mission is to break down barriers to mental wellness through education, community engagement, and innovative programs that empower individuals to take control of their mental health journey.

We strive to develop comprehensive resources and support systems that address the unique needs of each individual and community we serve.

Our Journey

Project Founded

January 2018

Project Mind Matters was established with a small team of passionate mental health advocates and professionals.

First Community Workshop

March 2018

Conducted our first community workshop focusing on stress management techniques for students.

School Program Launch

September 2019

Launched specialized mental wellness programs in 10 local schools, reaching over 5,000 students.

Digital Resource Platform

April 2020

In response to the pandemic, we developed a comprehensive online resource platform to provide support virtually.

National Recognition

November 2021

Received national recognition for our innovative approach to community mental health support.

Today

Present

Currently serving over 20,000 individuals annually through our various programs and initiatives.
