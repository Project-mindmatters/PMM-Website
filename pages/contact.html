Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to commonly asked questions about our services and programs

What services does Project Mind Matters offer?

Project Mind Matters offers a range of mental health and wellness services including workshops, support groups, counseling services, community events, and educational resources. Our programs are designed to cater to individuals, families, schools, and organizations.

How can I participate in your workshops or support groups?

You can register for our workshops and support groups through our Events page. Some programs require pre-registration, while others are open attendance. For specific inquiries about a program, please contact us directly.

Are your services free or is there a cost involved?

We offer a combination of free and paid services. Community events and certain support groups are often free, while specialized workshops and counseling services typically have associated fees. We also offer sliding scale payment options and scholarships for those facing financial hardship.

How can my organization collaborate with Project Mind Matters?

We welcome collaborations with organizations that share our vision for mental wellness. Please contact us through the form on this page with details about your organization and the nature of the potential collaboration. Our partnerships team will reach out to discuss possibilities.

Do you offer virtual/online services?

Yes, we offer a variety of virtual workshops, support groups, and counseling services. Our online programs provide the same quality support as our in-person services, making mental wellness resources accessible regardless of location.
